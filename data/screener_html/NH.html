About

Narayana Hrudayalaya Ltd is engaged in providing economical healthcare services. It has a network of multispecialty and super specialty hospitals spread across multiple locations.[1]

Key Points

Hospital Portfolio[1]
- India: 18 owned/operated hospitals, 2 heart centers, 20 clinics and dialysis centers. Jammu unit is removed and is considered as a part of discontinued operation effective FY25.
- Cayman Islands: 2 hospital.
** - Total:** 42 healthcare facilities with 5,554 operational beds (including Cayman).
