About

J.B. Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals Limited, established in 1976, is one of India's leading pharmaceutical companies. An integrated, publicly-listed organization with a focus on supplying affordable, quality products both in India and internationally, JBCPL is trusted by healthcare professionals globally. [1]

Key Points

Change in Management[1]
Torrent Pharmaceuticals Ltd. acquired control of the company through share purchase agreements executed in Jun and Jul'25, followed by an open offer under SEBI takeover regulations. The acquisition was completed in Jan and Feb'26, after which Torrent Pharma became the promoter of the company with a 48.8% equity stake as of Mar'26.
